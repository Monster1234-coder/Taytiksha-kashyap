Smart bin It's an automatic dustbin
that collects the waste lying
near the dustbin and take it inside the dustbin.
You must have often noticed that Whenever
you see a dustbin, there is a lot
of dust around it making the place
near the dustbin very unhygienic So, Smart bin can be a smart solution
for that This dustbin uses an ultrasonic sensor to
sense objects around the dustbin
and give command to an Arduino UNO
which give command to a servo
motor attached with a swiper that
swipes the waste lying near the dustbin
by moving 180Â° with the help of
this the place near the dustbin
become clean and hygenic.
